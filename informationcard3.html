Watering Basics for Houseplants

  1. Know Your Plant
    Different plants require different watering schedules.
  2. Check Soil Moisture
    Always feel the soil before watering to avoid overwatering.
  3. Use Proper Drainage
    Ensure pots have drainage holes to prevent root rot.
  4. Water Thoroughly
    Let water flow out from the bottom of the pot.
  5. Adjust by Season
    Plants need less water during cooler months.
